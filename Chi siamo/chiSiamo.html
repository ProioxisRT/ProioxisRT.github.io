    • The Beginning

    The project started with the selection of the team. The first members were Matteo Tasca and Samuele Vinante, already part of the Proioxis team. They were later joined by Alice Roverato, with experience in document writing, and Michele Gasparini, chosen for his expertise in marketing. Finally, Luca Ziroldi and Critian Rebasti were involved, due to their good time reaction. Proioxis was born from the fusion of experienced members, former rivals, and motivated newcomers—a diverse team with one shared mindset: this comeback is personal.

    Our LOGO

    We chose a minimalist, clean design inspired by the purity of a stylized Greek column. Using geometric shapes and bold, angular lines, we reinterpreted the machine’s complex geometry, breaking away from traditional patterns.

    Our NAME

    The name Proioxis RT reflects our identity as a team driven by determination, ambition, and a passion for competition. Proioxis, meaning “impetus of the battle” in Greek, symbolizes our relentless drive to push forward and surpass all limits. RT, short for Racing Team, highlights our competitive spirit and unity in the pursuit of excellence. Our name is not just a label — it is a true reflection of who we are.

    Our CAR

    Creating a machine capable of going below the 1.100-second mark.

    Our SLOGAN

    This slogan reflects our evolution. We’ve changed, we’ve grown, and we’ve worked hard to turn ideas into real, measurable results. We’re not the same team as before — we’re better, stronger, and ready to prove it.
