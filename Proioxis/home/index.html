    What Is F1 in Schools?

    F1 in Schools is a multidisciplinary challenge where student teams collaborate to design, develop, and race a miniature Formula 1 car powered by compressed air. Each team, made up of 3 to 6 students, takes on the full experience of running a Formula 1 team — from creating a unique identity and brand, to developing a communication strategy, business plan, and engineering their race car for competition. At stake is the incredible opportunity to represent Italy at the F1 in Schools World Finals.

    Driven to win

    This year, we are proud to have reached an important milestone by winning first place at the Triveneto Regional Championship Final A on May 11, 2025. This achievement has secured our place in the next stages of the competition. Motivated by this success, we continue to work with passion and determination toward our goal: winning the national finals.
